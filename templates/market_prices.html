Market Insights

Seasonal Price Patterns

Prices for most vegetables tend to be lower during their peak harvest seasons. Plan your planting schedule to potentially harvest when prices are historically higher.

Supply Chain Factors

Transportation costs, storage facilities, and middlemen can significantly impact final market prices. Consider direct-to-consumer selling options when possible.

Quality Premium

Organic and high-quality produce often commands a 15-30% price premium in urban markets. Consider certification if your practices already align with organic standards.
