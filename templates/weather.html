Weather-Based Farming Tips

Crop Selection

Choose crops that are well-suited to your local climate and seasonal weather patterns. Consider drought-resistant varieties in areas with limited rainfall.

Water Management

Implement efficient irrigation systems like drip irrigation. Collect rainwater during monsoon seasons for use during dry periods.

Crop Protection

Use weather forecasts to time pesticide and fungicide applications. Apply before rain to allow time for drying, but not immediately before heavy rainfall.

Planting Calendar

Develop a seasonal planting calendar based on historical weather patterns in your region. Adjust planting dates based on current weather forecasts.
